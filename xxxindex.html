The Gold Coast's Leading Music School

Discover Music Robina has been offering private music tuition since 1997. In our 18-year history we have mentored some of the Gold Coast's finest up and coming musicians.

The Best Teachers

Our teachers are all highly qualified, experienced, encouraging and enthusiastic. They are able to teach a variety of different styles so can assist you no matter what direction you, or your child, would like to take your music

Live Concerts

Twice yearly our students can show their new found skills at one of our music festivals.

We aim to create a fun, encouraging environment at our concerts, where students can enjoy performing without too much pressure.
